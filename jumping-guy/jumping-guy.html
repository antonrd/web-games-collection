Jumping Guy

How to Play

  • Jump with Space, shoot with X, pause/resume with P.
  • Jump over the brown rocks. Hitting them costs a life.
  • Collect yellow gems. 10 gems give you 5 bullets.
  • Bullets destroy rocks.
  • As you progress the game gets faster.
